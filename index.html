About Us

Mercury Studios is an affordable, photography studio complex in Amsterdam where we encourage creative individuals to bring their ideas to life. The studio space offers both natural and professional studio lighting; we specialise in portrait, editorial and fashion photography. It is a creative space where creative individuals can interact with each other and co-create. If requested, we can also provide photography workshops, fashion styling and a production team (make-up, fashion, etc.)

  • € 50 per hour (min. 2 hours)
